ON PROBLEMS 

Our choicest plans <br/>
have fallen through<br/>, 
our airiest castles<br/> 
tumbled over, <br/>
because of lines <br/>
we neatly drew <br/>
and later neatly <br/>
stumbled over. <br/>

- Piet Hein
